Overview

ReadabilityTool.com ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at readabilitytool.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Site is a free readability analysis tool. All text you enter into the tool is processed entirely within your browser and is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere.

Information We Collect

Text you enter into the tool: Any text you paste or type into the readability checker is processed locally in your browser using JavaScript. This text is never sent to any server, never stored, and never accessible to us. When you close or refresh the page, your text is gone.

Automatically collected information: Like most websites, we may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information when you visit the Site, including your browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This information is collected in aggregate and is not linked to any individual user.

Cookies: We use cookies as described in the Cookies section below.

Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this Site and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this Site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Analytics cookies: We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. These cookies collect information in aggregate form and do not identify individual users.
